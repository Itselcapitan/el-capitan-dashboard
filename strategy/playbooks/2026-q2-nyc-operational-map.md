## 1. Ticket Sales Strategy — Compete For Slot Position

The competition framing matters: your set time on July 17 and
August 1 is determined by ticket-sales volume vs. the other DJs on
each bill. Selling 20 tickets when the leading DJ sells 80 puts you
on at 9 PM to an empty room. Selling 60 when the leader sells 50
flips the bill in your favor. **The ticket-sales work IS the
booking strategy** — there's no separating promo from outcome here.

### The 50-14 day window
Start ticket promo 50+ days out for each gig; never shorter than 14
days. Promo links should come from Rose Gold Presents with
DJ-specific tracking codes so every sale is attributed correctly.

| Gig | Earliest promo start | Latest acceptable start |
|---|---|---|
| July 17 (Lyra Rooftop) | May 28, 2026 | July 3, 2026 |
| August 1 (Summer Boat Series) | June 12, 2026 | July 18, 2026 |

The May 28 date for the July 17 gig is **23 days from now**. Get
the tracking link from Rose Gold this week.

### The promo mix drop (highest-leverage single asset)
Record a 20-30 min "After the Bell" branded SoundCloud mix. Drop
exactly 1-2 weeks before each gig. Reasoning: gives the network
enough time to plan, not enough time to forget. Promote heavily
during this window.

For July 17 → drop the mix between July 3 and July 10.
For August 1 → drop between July 18 and July 25.

### Corporate network conversion (your unfair advantage)
The Wall Street network is your single biggest ticket-volume source.
Other DJs on the Rose Gold bills won't have a finance-bro distribution
channel. Specific tactics:

- **Office Slack / Group chats**: friends in finance, college alumni
  network, fraternity/sorority NYC chapters. Direct DM with the link
  is more effective than mass posts.
- **Early-bird tier exclusivity**: ask Rose Gold for a unique
  discount code you can give your network ("FINANCE10" or similar).
  Creates social currency for the recipients to share with their
  friends.
- **Corporate-perk framing**: position as "rooftop / boat party
  with my friends, free guest list adds if you bring 5+." Finance
  professionals respond to exclusivity + group dynamics.
- **No volume pricing**: don't drop the price floor; preserve full
  ticket revenue. Use comps for true VIP only.

### What to track in your dashboard

Add a new section under Campaigns or Outreach:
- `gigs/{gigId}/ticketsSold` (target: highest on the bill)
- `gigs/{gigId}/ticketsByChannel` (Slack, IG DM, in-person, etc.)
- `gigs/{gigId}/conversionByContact` (which network ties actually
  bought)

This tells you which channels are working and gives you a feedback
loop for the next round of gigs after July.
